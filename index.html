About US

At CTHealth Limited we are rethinking healthcare delivery by working alongside valued partners. Our dream is to bring healthcare closer to patients in a manner that is both cost-effective and abundant. We believe long and difficult waits to access quality healthcare is a gross violation of basic human rights.

CTHealth Limited aims to harness benefits from available technology to partner with like-minded local and global institutions to modernize healthcare delivery at a
national level in order to realize universal health coverage – “healthcare for all”. We work with healthcare professionals to reengineer existing healthcare delivery processes and help them reimagine a more effective, data-driven, knowledge centric set of integrated services. By building solutions that bridges the gap between current and future application needs,

CTHealth Limited hopes to develop platform agnostic healthcare delivery systems that medical and non-medical care providers find easy to use in order to reach out to as many in need.
