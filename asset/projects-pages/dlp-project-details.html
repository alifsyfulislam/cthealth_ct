Distance Portal Learning (DLP)

Join in the ONLINE certificate course on Dia-betology through Distance Learning Program (DLP) project run by Diabetic Association of Bangladesh (BADAS).

The course aims to create more skilled physicians to meet up the increasing demand of diabetes care in South-Asian countries.

  • Doctors can recieve the internationally accredited Certificate by learning the course online from anywhere.
  • Cost effective for doctors as physical presence is reduced.
  • Access to coordinator / teacher at all times
  • Students can check their real-time progress
  • The course is available through any electronic device
